PriorityApp

Priorities

Taking decisions has to be easy! Go ahead:

  1. Enter all the priorities you can think of.
  2. Click "Ready to go" when you've finished with the list.
  3. Order the priorities, most important on top.
  4. Click the button "Finish round" when you're ready. The last item will be fixed as being least priority.
  5. Repeat step 2 and 3 until you have all the priorities fixed
  6. Use the "Export" option to take away your results.
  7. Be happy with your decision! :)
